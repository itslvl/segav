BMKG

The Meteorology, Climatology and Geophysics Agency (abbreviated as BMKG), previously known as the Meteorology and Geophysics Agency (abbreviated as BMG) is a non-ministerial government agency in Indonesia which has the task of carrying out government duties in the fields of meteorology, climatology and geophysics.

BASARNAS

 (0341) 325443
The National Search and Rescue Agency or known as BASARNAS, is a non-ministerial government agency that carries out government duties in the field of search and rescue

About Segav

Segav is here as a comprehensive solution for travel planning, disaster emergency management, and reliable navigation. We also provide the latest information about the latest conditions in Malang City, so that residents can always follow the latest developments. If you want to contribute directly to social activities, we are also looking for volunteers. Find everything you need to stay connected and informed at Segav!

Why Segav

You should use the Segav website because we provide complete solutions for travel, disaster emergency management and navigation needs. With the latest information about Malang conditions, you can stay updated and ready to face any situation. Additionally, as a social platform, we welcome volunteer contributions, allowing you to participate directly in social activities that benefit society. With Segav, you not only get access to trusted information, but also have the opportunity to do good and make a positive impact on the community.
